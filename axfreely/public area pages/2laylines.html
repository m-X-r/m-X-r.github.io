The Parking Lot

You jump in your car and notice a flyer on your windshield. The brochure says Mr. Seafood, delicious seafood, in big, bold letters. Hum, you think seafood would be excellent. You decided to head out to get some.

At Mr. Seafood's you get a whitefish basket to go. While waiting to pull out the parking lot you see Sunshine Thrift Store across the street. Suddenly you remember needing a CD player for something. You pull into the thrift store, but your fish smells so good.




Go inside the thrift store real quick and, eat the fish when you get back. It would have cooled down some.

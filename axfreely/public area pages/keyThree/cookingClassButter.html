Monday Bites

You join in the Cooking Class. It just started. The Monday Bites as it’s called is a demo about sautéing with butter. The chef ends up making a pasta with sautéed onions, carrots and shrimp with a peppery creamy white sauce. Everyone got a taste at the end. Best part was that it was not only delicious but it wasn’t nearly as hard to make as you would have thought.

As the class wraps up you go to grab a recipe sheet. You drop it and when you bend down to pick it up you see that it landed on top of a flat round disk with a keyring hooked onto it. It looks very “fobbie,” For lack of a better word.




As they say, When in Rome finders keepers.
You place it on the counter closer to where the chef is cleaning up. It’s probably hers.

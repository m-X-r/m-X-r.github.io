The Lounge for Nursing Moms

You come to a door with a sign on it that says Nursing Mothers Lounge. Like it’s a “member’s only” bar or something. You also hear a noise, a low humming you want to investigate. You go to turn the nob when you see it has a red in use message on it. Like an airplane bathroom nob. Then you hear a voice from inside. “I’m pumping, come back later!” Shocked you stammer Sorry! And walk away.

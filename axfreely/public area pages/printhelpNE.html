The help desk

"You need to go to the kiosk behind me, scan your card, type in your pin, and select your print job." The librarian paused for a second to see if I got what she had just said. My eyes said no, so she continued. "You get ten free pages a day, and then it will be 25 cents per page after that." She pauses for another second and says ok and walks off to answer another question.

Stone Labyrinth

You walk down the hall. You start running your hand against the stone wall to your left. As hot as it is here, the wall is cool to the touch. A new hall appears perpendicular to you and you feel a slight, hot, sandy breeze against your face.




There is a deep arch in front of you. Does it lead to another hall?
There is a deep arch to the left of you. Does it lead to another hall or someplace else? There is a deep arch to the right of you. Where could it lead?
you feel a chill and turn to see a stone wall behind you where the hall you were walking down used to be.

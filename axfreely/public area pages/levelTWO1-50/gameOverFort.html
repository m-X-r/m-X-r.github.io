She explains to you that you need to reserve meeting rooms before using them. Lucky for you the room is available, but you can’t block yourself from view and you will need to take apart the wonderous fort you have made. You let her know that you were just about to take a nap after a long day of construction. "Sorry, no sleeping in the library." The Librarian replies. You crawl out from inside your makeshift fort and grab your jacket. Since you can’t have any fun at the library you decided to go home and make a fort in your living room.

The Printer

You turn to gather your bear photos. Right as you pick up the last one the printer starts printing again. “Those are mine” you hear the stranger say as they push past you to gather their prints. You see a glimpse of what looks to be a lady bodybuilder posing in a hammock. You look at the stranger. He looks at you. More prints print off and he quickly breaks eye contact to gather them all before anyone else sees.

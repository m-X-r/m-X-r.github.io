Photocopy Machine

A Xerox Machine! Now that’s old school. You wonder if the staff take dirty photocopies during after-hours parties.

You eye the coin return slot on the machine and can’t resist the urge to stick your finger in it to see if you strike gold. Then you feel it, a quarter! You look left and right, but no one seems to be interested in the copier or the quarter.

You pull the quarter out and gaze upon it. They do say every little bit counts.

Man with Furze

The two pig brothers next ran into a Man with Furze. For those who do not know, furze, is basically a bundle of sticks. When the brothers saw the furze the third pig was very unimpressed, but the second pig, he was ecstatic.

"Oh my! What lovely Furze!" The second brother exclaimed.

"Thank you so much!" The Man said, "you know, if you like it that much, I would be willing to give it to you if you wanted to build something with it."

"Really!" Shouted the second pig, tickled pinker than usual.

"Are you seriously going to build your house out of this furze! You are almost as bad as our brother if you do." The third pig exclaimed.

  • "First off, our brother is wonderful! Secondly, I totally am going to build my house out of this amazing furze, stop hating brother!" Stated the second pig, with his hand on his hip.
  • "I love this furze sooooo much! I can't build a house out of it, but I would love to build some bird houses for my future house out of it" Said the second pig.

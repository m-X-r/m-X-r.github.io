Path to Town

"Ok brother, stay safe!" yelled out the second pig, as he and his brother left the first pig to rest.

The first pig had a sound sleep that night. In the morning he was just about to make breakfast when he heard a knock at the door. "Who is it!" He shouted. "This is private property!"

In reply he heard a deep husky voice. "Private property my eye, this looks like the grocery store and I've come to get my breakfast, now let me in!"

The first pig looked out the window and saw a great wolf. "It's a wolf come to eat me he whispered to no one in particular, what should I do? Oh, I think so much better when my brothers are around."

While the first pig was trying to come up with a plan the wolf outside took a big breath and blew the straw house down.

"Oh My!" were the last words the first pig got out before the wolf ate him.

"Breakfast of champions!" The wolf shouted aloud to no one in particular.

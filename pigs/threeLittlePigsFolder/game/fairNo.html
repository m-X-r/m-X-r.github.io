Third Pigs House

The man pleaded with the pig, "oh come on! Trust me! You will love the fair! If you miss it you will regret it, that is a fact and a promise."

"I don't want to get eaten," the pig replied "I have so much more to do in life, including finding my fortune."

"We will be together, so either we both get eaten or we both do not get eaten. How about that?" The farmer tried to reason with the pig.

I guess it would be better getting eaten if I wasn't alone. Alright! I'll go!"

  • "You are going to love the fair!" The man shouted. Then him and the pig gave each other a high-five.

This is not my Hat

You are still looking towards the underwater forest when you see the big fish swim out, quickly and slowly at the same time. He was wearing his hat.

He paused and glanced in your direction and then swam away. You feel like he burped.

You float there staring at the forest entrance to see if the little fish would swim out.




. . . . .
. . . . . . . . . .
He never did.

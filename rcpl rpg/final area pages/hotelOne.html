Hotel Bruce

Suddenly you hear birds chirping and the sound of wind blowing through the trees. You feel the wind as well, does the wind blow in underwater storytime closets?

You look around and notice you are no longer in scuba gear underwater but instead in a woodland forest. You are sitting in some magic floating leather recliner.

It's the kind of chair you would find at fancy movie theaters, the kind where you can order food off a menu and have it delivered to you. There is a large coke in one armrest cup holder and a large bucket of buttered popcorn in your lap. Suddenly you hear a booming voice in your ears.

"Bruce is back, and this time he has more than goslings on his plate! A host of woodland creatures has taken over his den, and they just won't go away."

Suddenly you see Hotel Bruce, by Ryan T. Higgins, floating in the sky in big bubble letter script.

This is not my Hat

"No!"

The fish says.

"Asleep! He probably won't wake up for a long time!" He says with glee.

You start to tell him that the fish is, in fact, awoken, but he cuts you off again.

"Even if he does wake up, he probably won't notice that his hat is gone."

At this, you glance back towards the big fish. You see him noticing that his hat is gone. Before you can say anything, the little fish distracts you.

He starts swimming in showy circles in front of your face striking a pose to show off the hat at the tops and bottoms of the shape.

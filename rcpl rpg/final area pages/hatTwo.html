This is not my Hat

Instead of the underworld, you find that you are actually in the ocean with a scuba suit, goggles, and an oxygen tank. You find yourself in a Finding Nemo type environment like an ocean shelf. With coral reefs and other wavy flowy underwater shrubbage.

Before you can ponder on the fact that you don't know how to scuba dive, a small fish with a bowler hat swims into your eye line. He looks at you and then says,

"This hat is not mine. I just stole it."




Ahhhh! Fish! Talk! Human!

A Grand Viking Gathering Hall

You walk into a large room with tables in the middle. It seems to be a Viking Kings Court.

There are animal fur rugs and wooden, no, animal bone chandeliers. Who lights all these candles, you think.

Suddenly you are grabbed from behind. Next thing you know, you have been slung across the room. You hit something hard and fall into a tumble, somehow rolling to your feet like a ninja.

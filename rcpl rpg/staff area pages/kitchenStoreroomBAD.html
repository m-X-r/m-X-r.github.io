Kitchen Store Room

You rush through the door to the West of you. The room you enter is full of containers.

Some have beans and flour in them. You see preserved jellies and jams, loaves of bread and vegetables, and preserved meats. In the torchlight, you even see a round of cheese.

Outside the room, in the hall, you hear a conversation among multiple people. You just missed them. Can't go back out that way, now what?

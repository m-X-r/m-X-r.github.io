Bosses Office

You head back towards the Branch Managers Office. He's standing in front of the door. Why would you go this way!

"Oh, did you need something else?" He asks, imposing over you like a Viking King.

"Right." You stammer, "where are the lockers again?" That is the only thing you can think to say.

The Branch Manager just points, giving you an odd look. You turn to head in the direction he mentioned. Then you remember you just put that note in the locker.

"Ha, ha, I didn't mean locker. Why would I mean locker! I just put a note in my good friend Jennif, I mean Jessicas locker. Sometimes I call her Jennifer as a kid name. Ha, ha,...Ha, ha. Chair?"

At this point, you are sweating. The Branch Manager moves his arm a bit, still just pointing, looming, and odd-looking over you.

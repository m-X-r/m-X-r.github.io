Fifth Main Hall

You are creeping along, on your tippy-toes, like a cartoon character peeking behind you. Perhaps you should have been peaking in front because you run directly into a Library Viking.

In the torchlight, you see them in business casual clothing. But also with a giant shield strapped to their back and horned helmet of some kind.

They had a computer printout in their hand and were not looking where they were going either. So they are just a startled by the collision as you are.

That gives you just enough time to run away.




Run!

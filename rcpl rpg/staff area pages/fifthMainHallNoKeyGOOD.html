Fifth Main Hall

You walk into what seems to be a drive-thru area. Well, at least there is a drive-thru window.

There are two librarians in this new area, one at the window and one at a computer by the window. The librarian manning the drive-thru window speaks to you. "Did you need something?" They ask.

Yes, you say, I was looking for the Culinary Arts Coordinator, have you seen her? Both the librarians say, No. Then they both suggest that you email her to set up a time to meet. That seems to be the consensus, you say.

"Well," the one at the computers says, "you are welcome to use our public computers to email her. You can head out this door." Suddenly there is a door next to you that you didn't notice when you walked back.

Was that door always there, you ponder as the librarian gently pushes you out of it.

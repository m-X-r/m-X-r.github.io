Kitchen, the Staffside

You look around and see a light switch. Then a crazy idea pops into your head.

Figuring you would risk it, you start flashing the lights and making low moaning sounds. The librarian looks up at the lights, lets out a loud "NOPE," and walks out the kitchen via the wall they were trying to lock up.

Not wanting to go the same way they did, you try the door to the store-room. You discover that it locked behind you.

You walk over to the open wall and peek out to see if you see anyone before you head out.

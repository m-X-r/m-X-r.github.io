Janitors Closet

You dash into the door and run into a sink. Good thing it nocks the wind out of you because you hear talking in the hall, beyond the door.

"Did you hear that?" One voice asks

Another answers,"yeah, I heard it too. Perhaps we should check on the Branch Manager. Is he in his office?"

You hear a doorknob, thankfully not yours, jiggle. "Is this door usually locked?" you hear someone say.

"Yes, I think so."

"Well, let me dance a magic jig? and by that, I mean unlock it... wait, where are my keys?"

" Ha, you are not funny. Let us check on the Manager. He probably has a key."

"You are wrong. I'm hilarious, and the Manager will agree with me."

You hear footsteps walking away from you. That was close, you think, as you crack open the door and peek out. You head out, definitely going in the opposite direction.

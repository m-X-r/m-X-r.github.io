Photocopy Machine

A Xerox Machine! that feels just as old as a fax machine to you. I wonder if the staff take dirty photocopies during after-hours parties, you think to yourself.

Then you catch a glisten out of the corner of your eye. It's a quarter, you look left and right, but no one seems to be interested in the copier or the quarter.

I might take that, you think. They do say every little bit counts.

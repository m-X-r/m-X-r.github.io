A Kitchen in a Library?

You walk back to the lady sliding the white panels. Hey, is that a stove? You ask.

"Sure is!" She replies, "We just finished up a cooking class. Be sure to check the website or the whiteboard upfront to see when our next class is."

She smiles and closes herself behind the white paneling. Cooking classes, fancy, you say to yourself in a french (Esq) accent.

You hear giggling from behind the panel. Oh, you say, I didn't think anyone was listening. "No problem, have a nice day," you hear come back at you from behind the panel.

Books on Dispaly

You see a bunch of books displayed on a table. In the middle of the table is a fancy sign that says Fresh Picks. What’s a fresh pick? You say it mostly to yourself but someone next to you answers.

"Fresh Picks all have this yellow sticker on the spine."

An older man next to you says, pointing at one of the yellow dot shaped stickers.

"That means it’s a fresh pick. You can’t put them on hold, or renew them, but it’s the best way to get a new popular book quicker than being on hold for it."

Oh, neat, thanks, you say. The old man kind of grunts and moves to look at the fresh picks on the other side of the table. You see a book with an interesting title by someone named Agatha Christie.

The old man sees you looking, "that’s a good one, just finished reading that one. Great for mystery," he says. Really, you reply, I’ll hold on to this, maybe I'll to check it out later you tell him.

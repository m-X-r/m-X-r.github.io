Book Drop Room

You just walked in the front door, and now you are in a weird front door room.

There is a book drop to the left you along with a little stool. That's all there is in this space, just a book drop and a stool. There should at least be a coat rack and maybe an umbrella stand.

Before you can finish your thought, someone comes in the door behind you and asks you to move. They have a bag of books to drop into the dropbox, and you are in their way.

Guess that's why the space is so empty, you think as you walk off.

Is16 and living her best life. She is a sophmore at a majority human high school. Other than humans they have Pennelope and a brown bear named Rosco. Rosco is not adjusting well, but since Pennelope has been in school with humans since elementry school she has learned to not only survive but thrive at her all human school.

The last time Pennelope had trouble fitting in was when she was in the first grade and just starting school. She found humans to be delicious so she kept trying to eat all her classmates. Luckly, she didnt have all her teeth in yet and could only swallow them so she was able to spit them out after trying to eat them. All her classmates were "eaten" and they all came out unharmed, except for the dino spit and the emotional trauma of being swallowed. No matter how hard she tried, she could not stop herself from eating her classmates. That is until one day her tail got to close to the goldfish bowl and the goldfish tried to eat her. Goldfish dont have teeth so Pennelope was unharmed, except for the goldfish spit and the emotional trauma of being tasted. Up unitl that point no one knew that some goldfish find T Rexe's delicious. After that experience Pennelope and her classmates bonded over swaping traumatic attempted consumption stories. Pennelope's people cravings also stopped for the most part, anytime she thinks of eating a person now she thinks of goldfish and that nips that craving in the bud.

At her high school Penelope has become quite popular. She tries to reach out to Rosco every now and then to try and help with his tranition, but hes not ready yet. Good thing is that Penelope is presestant and she refuses to give up on him. Her persistance has helped her alot at school. She makes good grades, she is a cheerleader and the top gymnast in school, and she has plenty of friends. In fact Penelope is somewhat of an "it" girl at her school.

Fifth Key Point

"The wolf then invited the pig to come and get turnips with him the following evening. The pig agreed but left earlier than the appointed time and got the turnips home before the wolf showed up."

Now for this point we get to add some new map! No more Pig Houses! We must decide where the Turnip Field is in relation to the Third Pigs House.

Path to Town

"Well, we can't just build our house in the path to town." The third pig said, "so where are we going to build it?"

"How about just off the path, right next to that lake front. It will provide plenty of mud for our bricks!" The Man exclaimed pointing West.

"I like it!" The third pig said, "all that free mud is going to make great bricks."

"We will make the sturdiest house around." The Man added.

The pig and the Man smiled at each other as they set off towards the West.

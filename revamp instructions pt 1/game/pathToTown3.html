Path to Town

"Well, you can just build your house in the path to town." The third pig said, "so where are you going to build it?"

"Just off the path, duh!" The first pig exclaimed.

"How about over there?" The second pig chimed in pointing West. "It looks nice and flat over there, great place to build a house."

"A flimsy house." The third pig added.

"You know what brother, oh, I mean my real and truest brother. I agree with you, that spot to the West looks perfect." The first pig said while giving the third pig some side eye.

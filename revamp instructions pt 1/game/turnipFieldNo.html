The Turnip Field

The next day at 12 noon, the third pig and the man walked to the turnip field. They collected so many turnips! When they got home, they started a turnip stew, they prepared some turnips to be dried, they canned some turnips, they even roasted a few. "Man, that tip about the turnip field was the best!" Said the man.

"Right! I really hope he comes back tomorrow with another great tip." said the pig.

"By the way," the man said, "you do realize he was only trying to get you in that field to eat you right?"

"Never trust a wolf," the pig said, "typically they are only trying to eat you. Everyone knows that!"

"Everyone does." Replied the man.

The man and the pig gave each other a high five and then they ate the most delicious turnip stew.

  • The man and the pig turned in for the night.

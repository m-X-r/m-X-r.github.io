You come across a table set for tea. You hear a rustling behind you and encounter a Ursus arctos with a top hat and a bow tie on. You would run but you are distracted by the steam coming out the spout of the beautiful teapot that the Ursus is holding. Suddenly you hear a soothing voice in your head. It's kind and warm when it says, Hi dear. I would invite you to tea but I forgot my milk and sugar. I put them in a box in the cold dark part of the cave. Would you be a dear and get them for me?
